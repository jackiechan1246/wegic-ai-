What is Wegic?

Wegic is your AI website team—your AI designer, developer, and manager. Just chat, and Wegic will help you create custom websites in seconds, publish with one click, and manage updates automatically—no coding required.

Can I try Wegic for free?

Yes, Wegic offers a free plan to help you get started. However, if you need additional credits or advanced features like a custom domain, you'll need to upgrade to the Premium plan to unlock the full experience.

Why do I need to create a website?

A website helps establish your online presence, allowing you to reach people worldwide. It also builds trust, showing that your brand or business is legitimate. A website strengthens your brand whether you're creating a blog, business site or portfolio.

How can I create my own website?

With Wegic, creating a website is as simple as chatting with AI. Just describe the type of site you need, provide the website name, select your style and language, and Wegic will instantly generate a custom website for you. It also offers AI editing, custom URL options, and one-click publishing, so you can launch your site effortlessly.

Can I create websites without coding knowledge?

Absolutely! Wegic requires no coding skills. Simply provide your requirements, and Wegic AI handles the technical work, helping you create a beautiful, functional website without writing a single line of code.
